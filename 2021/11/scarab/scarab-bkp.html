ScarabNFT is a collection of 10,000 algorithmically generated NFT tokens created on Hathor Network. Despite the fact that the sale of ScarabNFTs is a step towards the full-scale ScarabGame development, ScarabNFTs are not just random images, but rather a trace of being previously involved in the Scarab ecosystem, since ScarabNFTs can be used on their own in a number of ways:

1.Winning a Community Giveaway: each ScarabNFT holder will get a community giveaway of a certain value based on the number of ScarabNFT in the holder's wallet. 1 NFT = 1 giveaway, 2 NFT = 2giveaways, 3 NFT = 3 giveaways and so on. In addition, the holders of more than 10 and 100 NFT will receive extra rewards. We believe that early adopters and the long-term supporters of the project should always be rewarded.

2. Access to the ScarabGame early Beta. The ScarabGame Beta testers will also be granted a free starter pack when the game is launched on the main network.

3.Access to the incentivazed ScarabDeFi test network following its launch.

4.ScarabNFTs will be used in the ScarabGame (staking and management, as well as in-game bonuses and usage.

Rarity:

ScarabNFTs are randomly generated from the following elements with a varying rarity percentage:

1. An object held in the upper jaws.

2. Limbs.

3. A gem embedded in the head.

4. Wings.

5. Body.

6. A pattern on the scarab's shell and head.

7. An object underneath the beetle.

8. Background.

9. An object held in the hind limbs.

A custom Scarab:

several scarabs will be drawn completely by hand in a certain distinctive style. They will be extremely rare, since there will be no more than 10 images of them. Each scarab will have its own name, making it even more unique and worth collecting. Several of them will be given away to the community members during our promo campaign. You can find more information about it below. The rest of the Scarabs will be available for purchase among all other NFTs, so the chances of obtaining such a Scarab are equal for everyone. The lucky holders of custom scarabs will receive extra rewards in community giveaways.
